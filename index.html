Activities

Ashley and Ben love the spending time together outdoors which is why we love all of the outdoor space that this venue provides. Please feel free to bring your lawn games and lawn chairs. The park has soccer and lacrosse fields (great wide open spaces) as well as a frisbee golf course.

If you're not from the area and you'll have kids with you, we would highly recommend venturing over to check out Sibley Park before you leave town as it is just a one-mile drive away from the pavilion. Their "Sibley Farm" area within Sibley Park has a petting zoo and a large playground with a farming theme. Kids will love it!

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there bathrooms on-site?

Yes, the facility has modern indoor plumbing.

Does the pavilion have air conditioning?

Yes, it is unlikley that it will be needed, but in the event that we get a scorching hot day at the end of May, the pavilion will be air conditioned.

Where should I park?

The main parking lot adjacent to the building has 33 spaces, if this begins to fill up we ask that this parking lot be used only for loading/unloading as well as for folks who have a harder time getting around. There is additional parking just a few hundred feet further down the road.

Are dogs allowed?

Dogs are allowed in the park, but it is recommended that they be on a leash unless they are able to stay near the venue on their own without chasing after the wildlife (primarily squirrels).

Can I bring my own drinks?

We think we'll have a little bit of something for everyone, but you are welcome to bring whatever drinks you would like, the only limitation is that the park does not allow glass bottles or hard liquor.

Doesn't Land of Memories Park sometimes flood in the spring?

Unfortunately, yes. When the Minnesota River water level is high the park is known to flood. Even when it does flood the pavilion is usually still accessible since it is perched up on a hill a bit higher than the rest of the park. If flooding occurs to the point where the venue is no longer accessible, this website will provide you with updated information on an alternate location.
