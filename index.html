Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to RSVP?

Nope, we don't need any firm commitments. If you can make it, great! If not, that's okay too.

Are there bathrooms on-site?

Yes, the facility has modern indoor plumbing.

Does the pavilion have air conditioning?

Yes, it has A/C and heat. In the event of rain it has a large indoor garaged area where lawn games can be setup.

Where should I park?

For those that have a harder time getting around there is limited parking on the side of the building. For everyone else, we would recommend loading/unloading at the venue and then parking at one of the many spaces available around the park.

Are dogs allowed?

Sorry, unfortunately dogs are not allowed in Sibley Park.

Can I bring my own drinks?

We think we'll have a little bit of something for everyone, but you are welcome to bring whatever drinks you would like, the only limitation is that the park does not allow glass bottles or hard liquor.
